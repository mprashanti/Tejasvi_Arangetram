The Dancer

Tejasvi Vijay’s journey in Bharatanatyam has been one of deep dedication and artistic discovery. From her first steps as a child to the complex rhythms of this debut performance, she has embraced the rich heritage of South Indian classical dance.

This Arangetram marks a pivotal moment in her artistic life—a completion of her initial training and the beginning of her path as a solo artist.

The Guru

Tejasvi has been trained under the guidance of Guru Srimathi Parvatha Chidambaram, whose commitment to the purity of the art form has shaped her performance today.

The relationship between Guru and Shishya is the foundation of this art. Through years of mentorship, Tejasvi has learned to translate rhythm into grace and storytelling into emotion.

Blessings

This arangetram is offered at the lotus feet of our beloved spiritual guides.

Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba
